Frequently Asked Questions

How do I withdraw my funds? ▼

You can withdraw funds anytime through your dashboard. Go to Investments > Withdraw, select your amount, and choose your payment method. Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Make sure you've completed KYC verification first.

What's the minimum deposit amount? ▼

The minimum deposit is $100 for all investment plans. You can invest up to any amount that matches your chosen plan tier. We accept cryptocurrency, bank transfers, and credit cards.

How long does KYC verification take? ▼

KYC verification typically takes 24-48 hours. We require a valid ID, proof of address, and a selfie for verification. Submit your documents through Dashboard > KYC > Upload Documents. You'll receive an email confirmation once verified.

How are investment returns calculated? ▼

Investment returns are calculated based on market performance and credited to your account balance periodically at 23:59 UTC. Your balance updates automatically, and you can withdraw when eligible. Returns vary based on market conditions and investment plan - capital at risk.

How is my account secured? ▼

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS), two-factor authentication (2FA), and secure password hashing. Your funds are stored in segregated accounts and covered by insurance. Enable 2FA immediately in your Account Settings > Security.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my investment plan? ▼

Yes! You can upgrade to a higher plan anytime through Investments > Plans. Only the additional amount needs to meet the new minimum. Downgrades take effect at the end of your investment period. Contact support if you need assistance.

How does the referral program work? ▼

Share your referral link and earn 5% commission on each friend's first deposit. Unlimited earning potential! Find your link at Account > Referrals. You'll receive notifications when your referrals earn returns.

Do I need to report my earnings for taxes? ▼

Yes, investment returns may be subject to tax depending on your country of residence. We provide detailed transaction statements for your records. Consult with a tax professional about your obligations. Download reports from Dashboard > Reports.

1Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our investment services and comply with regulatory requirements. The categories of personal data we may collect include:

Personal & Identity Information

  • Full name, date of birth, and nationality
  • Email address, phone number, and physical address
  • Government-issued identification (passport, driver's licence, national ID)
  • Selfie / live photo for identity verification

Financial Information

  • Investment history, transaction records, and account balances
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses used for deposits
  • Bank or payment method details for withdrawals
  • Source of funds documentation (where required by AML regulations)

Technical Information

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Login timestamps and platform activity logs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Account creation, KYC verification, processing investments, deposits, and withdrawals
  • Legal compliance: Anti-money laundering (AML) checks, sanctions screening, and regulatory reporting
  • Security: Fraud detection, account protection, and incident investigation
  • Communication: Transaction notifications, account alerts, and customer support
  • Improvement: Platform analytics, performance monitoring, and feature development
  • Marketing: Promotional communications (only with your consent; unsubscribe available at any time)

3Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in limited circumstances:

  • Regulatory authorities: Where required by law (e.g., FCA, HMRC, SARS, or other financial regulators)
  • Service providers: Identity verification services, payment processors, and cloud infrastructure providers bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with prior notice to affected users)
  • Fraud prevention: Sharing data with law enforcement or fraud prevention agencies where legally required
  • With your consent: Any other sharing only occurs with your explicit prior consent

4Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data:

  • 256-bit TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for admin access
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Geographically distributed, ISO 27001-compliant data centres
  • Employee background checks and mandatory confidentiality agreements
  • Documented incident response plan for security breaches

Despite our best efforts, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please report any suspected security incidents to security@elitewealthcapita.uk.

5Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations:

  • Account & identity data: Duration of relationship plus 7 years (regulatory requirement)
  • Transaction records: 7 years from the date of each transaction
  • Communication logs: 3 years from the date of last contact
  • KYC documents: 5 years after account closure
  • Marketing preferences: Until you withdraw consent or close your account

When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

6Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. Contact us to exercise any of these rights:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Deletion

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations).

Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

Objection

Object to certain processing activities (e.g., direct marketing).

Restriction

Request that we limit how we process your data.

To exercise your rights, email privacy@elitewealthcapita.uk. We will respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK).

7Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform:

  • Essential cookies: Required for platform functionality (login sessions, CSRF protection)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language, theme, and other settings
  • Security cookies: Assist in fraud detection and account protection

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner. Disabling essential cookies may impact your ability to use the platform.

8International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your home jurisdiction where we or our service providers operate. We ensure that all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws by:

  • Using Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Transferring only to countries with adequate data protection standards
  • Maintaining contractual protections with all third-party processors

9Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us immediately at privacy@elitewealthcapita.uk and we will delete the data promptly.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • A prominent notice within your account dashboard
  • An updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page

Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
